Welcome to

Beep & Colorbar

Beep & Colorbar is a production house under which shorts films, featured films are taken. The contents of this production house highlight human emotion management, women empowerment, and related psychological issues that are often ignored in our society. The incidents portrayed are very common to our day to day life often about which we don't feel comfortable to talk about. The contents so far are released in independent platforms like Youtube under the direction of Samiron. The lead cast of most of the work, Mrittika Dey Sarkar, receives the "Best Actress" award in Peak City Film Festival for 'Mother' which is soon going to release on YouTube.On that note keep following for more such short films where viewers are able to connect their lives with the areas thrown light upon.
