Waiting Room

A story which highlights the harsh truth of how a war of independence and fight for power destroys many families, life and separates them from their loved ones.

The movie opens with a big extended family scene where everyone is having a gala time since Diwali is approaching and people have already started decorating the interior of the Then suddenly Shree comes to know her beloved, Srijan is returning back to home for the occasion to spend time with the entire family.Then we find both the lovers unite and their chemistry keeps developing to such extent that people started getting scared of Shree because Srijan will have to leave the place soon. Then Shree finally comes across the news of Srijan's departure which stabs her feelings and makes her turn pale with broken thoughts. Srijan sacrifices his love for Shree and marries a girl, Suparna to keep her father's word in death bed. Then after years war of Burma takes place heading towards the failure of democracy and the arrival of military rule in Myanmar in the year 1962.This is the time when Shree loses her family in the war and decides to leave the place with her maidservant. Shree was waiting in the "waiting room" of the station for the arrival of train where she meets her long parted lover Srijan like after a decade, when she learns that Srijan has married unlike Shree and settled down in his life with a lady, Suparna and they named their daughter 'Shree', as he narrates he could never get over this name.They again parted their ways with a heavy heart.
